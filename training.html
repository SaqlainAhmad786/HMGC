1-Day Organic Farming Workshop

Join us for an immersive 1-day workshop designed for anyone interested in learning the fundamentals of organic farming. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your gardening skills, this hands-on experience will cover essential topics like soil health, composting, natural pest control, and sustainable farming techniques.

During the workshop, you'll get practical knowledge on how to grow your own fruits and vegetables organically, with guidance from our expert farmers. By the end of the day, you'll be equipped with the tools and knowledge to start your own organic garden, ensuring healthier produce and a greener planet.

Workshop Highlights:

  • Understanding soil health and organic fertilization
  • Composting techniques for natural nourishment
  • Pest management without chemicals
  • Sustainable farming practices for small or large gardens

3-Day Basic Organic Farming Course

Our 3-day basic course offers a comprehensive introduction to organic farming, perfect for beginners and aspiring farmers. Through in-depth sessions and hands-on practice, you will learn the core principles of sustainable agriculture and organic cultivation methods.

Course Overview:

  • 1. Learn the importance of soil health, composting techniques, and how to enrich your soil with natural nutrients.
  • 2. Discover organic planting methods, crop rotation, and how to manage pests naturally without harmful chemicals.
  • 3. Pest management without chemicals
  • 4.Explore best practices for harvesting, storing, and maintaining an organic farm, along with insights into sustainable farm management.

By the end of the course, you’ll have the skills and confidence to start your own organic garden or farm, ensuring a healthy, eco-friendly way of growing produce.

We use eco-friendly practices to grow quality produce while protecting the environment and preserving biodiversity.
